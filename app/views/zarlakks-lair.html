Zarlakk's lair

He lies there, a great green dragon curled around an obsidian pillar, carved with dark sigils that glow faintly.

The scents of smoke and char and ash hang in the air. A thick miasma of menace and maliciousness.

Zarlakk notices your approach and seems to smile as he slowly uncoils the full extent of his body.

"What took you so long?" a voice echoes about your mind, but all you can hear is the bass rumble of the dragon's breath.
