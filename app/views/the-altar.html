The altar

You find yourself standing on thick marble flags that make up a circle.

Intricate tiled inlays and patterns dance their way in sleek curves and arcs towards a majestic centre-piece - an elaborate altar of a pale white stone that glows softly.

Carvings adorn the sides of the altar. You follow them round and a story emerges - a dark beast, a star falling to earth, a radiant maiden - a tale from a time long past.
