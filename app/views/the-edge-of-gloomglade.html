The edge of Gloomglade

Your first impressions of this "haunted forest" are somewhat underwhelming it has to be said.

The trees gently rise up around easy slopes, the space between them thick with lush ferns and the moist mulch from which they sprout. It's a picturesque sight as you wind your way through the natural path amidst the foliage until...wait...there in the bushes.

A note hastily written in a frantic, panicked hand reads "Beware Kel-Ratha! Beware the assassin of the seven portals!" You shudder, but decide to press on, heedful of the warning.

Eventually, you come to a crossroads of sorts.
